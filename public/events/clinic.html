Pave The Jungle

1st Annual Track Clinic

34 Madrid Kindergarten through 7th grade students completed a week long track & field clinic that concluded on August 7th with a multi-event competition at MadridHigh School.

Kindergarten through 2nd grade students competed in the 100 Meter Dash, Shot Put, and Long Jump with Emma Olsen and Gabe Peterson winning their division. 3rd through 4th grade participated in the 100 Meter Dash, Shot Put, High Jump and Long Jump with Grace Peterson and Eric Steig winning the division. 5th thru 7th grade competed in the 100 Meter Dash, Shot Put, High Jump, Long Jump and 200 Meter Dash and Rylan Santi and Michael Santi won their respective divisions.

The clinic raised $500 and all proceeds have been donated to the M Foundation and Pave the Jungle, which is a capital campaign raising funds for an all-weather track for the Madrid community.

Emma Olsen and Gabe Peterson winners of the kindergarten through 2nd grade divisions.

Grace Peterson and Eric Steig winners of the 3rd through 4th grade divisions.

Rylan Santi and Michael Santi winners of the 5th through 7th grade divisions.
