Pave The Jungle

Standing Hampton at Snus Hill Winery

A group of Madrid Jr. High/High School parents came together and brought the band Standing Hampton to Snus Hill Winery in August as a fundraiser/kick-off event for Pave the Jungle. The money raised, about $2800 was donated to the new committee, The M Foundation, which is working towards a goal of raising $500,000 for new track facilities in Madrid. Everyone enjoyed great music, food and wine.
