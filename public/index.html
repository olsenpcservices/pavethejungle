Pave The Jungle

Welcome to Pave the Jungle!

Pave the Jungle is an initiative started by the M Foundation. The objective of Pave the Jungle is to fund and build a state-of-the-art track and field facility to benefit the students of the Madrid Community Schools and the members of the Madrid community.



Mission

The mission of the M Foundation is to secure, manage, and provide resources for the students of the Madrid Community School District with the purpose of maintaining, enhancing, and promoting personal excellence in education, athletics, and the arts.
